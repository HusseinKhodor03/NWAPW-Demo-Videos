Rogue-Like Game

This is a project for NWAPW, and it is a single player rogue-like style game.

This is a rouge-like game where a chemical outbreak has occurred and infected the animals and people in the surrounding area. You (the player) are a scientist who must fight the mutants and work your way to the laboratory at the end of the map to stop the outbreak and save the world, or die trying.
